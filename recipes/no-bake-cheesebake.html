No Bake Cheesecake

Description

Quick and easy no bake cheesecake recipe that I can't seen to be able to find anymore.

Ingredients

  • Graham cracker crust
  • 8 oz cream cheese; softened
  • 8 oz Cool Whip
  • 1/3 cup sugar

Steps

  1. Mix cream cheese and sugar
  2. Fold in Cool Whip
  3. Pour filling into crust
